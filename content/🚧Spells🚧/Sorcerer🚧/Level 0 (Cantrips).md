# Cantrip Descriptions
## Chill Touch
**Level:** Cantrip  
**Mana Cost:** 0  
**Range:** Touch  
**Target:** One creature  
**Duration:** Instant

**Effect:** Cold damage that prevents healing.

|Class Tier|Damage|Additional Effect|
|---|---|---|
|Apprentice|1d8 + Spellcasting modifier|Target cannot heal for 1 round|
|Journeyman|2d8 + Spellcasting modifier|Target cannot heal for 1 round|
|Expert|2d8 + Spellcasting modifier|Target cannot heal for 2 rounds|
|Master|3d8 + Spellcasting modifier|Target cannot heal for 2 rounds|
|Legendary|4d8 + Spellcasting modifier|Target cannot heal for 3 rounds|

## Illusory Sound
**Level:** Cantrip  
**Mana Cost:** 0  
**Range:** 10 hexes  
**Target:** Within range  
**Duration:** Instant

**Effect:** Create minor auditory illusions and sound effects at target location.

|Class Tier|Additional Effect|
|---|---|
|Apprentice|Simple sounds (voices, animal noises, objects)|
|Journeyman|Simple sounds, can throw voice to different location|
|Expert|Complex sounds, can mimic specific voices heard before|
|Master|Complex sounds, can create multiple simultaneous sounds|
|Legendary|Perfect mimicry, can create conversations between multiple voices|

## Mage Hand
**Level:** Cantrip  
**Mana Cost:** 0  
**Range:** 6 hexes  
**Target:** One small object  
**Duration:** Sustained

**Effect:** Telekinetically manipulate small objects at range.

**Critical Effect:** Duration becomes 5 minutes instead of Sustained (no concentration required).

| Class Tier | Weight Limit | Additional Effect                                        |
| ---------- | ------------ | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| Apprentice | 5 lbs        | Basic manipulation                                       |
| Journeyman | 10 lbs       | Basic manipulation                                       |
| Expert     | 15 lbs       | Can manipulate multiple small objects up to weight limit |
| Master     | 20 lbs       | Can perform fine manipulation (writing, lockpicking)     |
| Legendary  | 30 lbs       | Can exert significant force (opening heavy doors)        |

## Minor Shield
**Level:** Cantrip  
**Mana Cost:** 0  
**Range:** Self  
**Target:** Self  
**Duration:** Until triggered or 1 minute

**Effect:** Brief damage reduction against next attack.

**Critical Effect:** Affects double the number of attacks.

|Class Tier|DR Bonus|Additional Effect|
|---|---|---|
|Apprentice|+2 DR|1 attack|
|Journeyman|+3 DR|1 attack|
|Expert|+3 DR|2 attacks|
|Master|+4 DR|2 attacks|
|Legendary|+5 DR|3 attacks|
