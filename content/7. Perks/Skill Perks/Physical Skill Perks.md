# Physical Skill Perk Descriptions
## Apprentice Tier
### Adrenaline Junkie
**Prerequisites:** Athletics 40+
**Effect:** Advantage when attempting dangerous physical stunts.
### Cat's Grace
**Prerequisites:** Agility 40+
**Effect:** Reduce falling damage by {Level} points and always land on your feet.
### Contortionist
**Prerequisites:** Agility 40+
**Effect:** Fit through any opening larger than your head.
### Human Heater
**Prerequisites:** Endurance 40+
**Effect:** Resist 2 Cold damage.
### Iron Lungs
**Prerequisites:** Endurance 40+
**Effect:** Hold your breath for 5x longer than normal and are immune to airborne toxins.
### Iron Stomach
**Prerequisites:** Endurance 40+
**Effect:** Eat anything without getting sick, gain nutrition from weird sources.
### Poison Resistance
**Prerequisites:** Endurance 40+
**Effect:** Advantage on saves vs poisons (doesn't stack with Nature version).
### Showoff
**Prerequisites:** Agility 40+
**Effect:** Advantage when performing acrobatics in front of an audience.
### Unbreakable
**Prerequisites:** Endurance 40+
**Effect:** When reduced to 0 HP, make an Endurance check to stay conscious for 1 round.
## Journeyman Tier
### Acrobat
**Prerequisites:** Agility 50+
**Effect:** Move through enemy-occupied spaces without provoking reactions. Additionally, you can use your momentum to perform impossible maneuvers like running across liquid surfaces or bouncing off walls to change direction mid-fall.
### Dramatic Entrance
**Prerequisites:** Agility 50+
**Effect:** Enter a scene in a physically dramatic way to gain everyone's attention. On a successful Agility check, gain Advantage on Influence checks with everyone who witnessed your entrance.
### Graceful Landing
**Prerequisites:** Agility 50+
**Effect:** Take half damage from falls and always land on your feet dramatically.
### One Punch
**Prerequisites:** Athletics 50+
**Effect:** Hard Success on an unarmed attack potentially knocks the target unconscious.
### Reactive Roll
**Prerequisites:** Agility 50+
**Effect:** Stand up from prone using 1 RP as a reaction.
### Strongman
**Prerequisites:** Athletics 50+
**Effect:** You can lift and carry 3x normal weight limits. Additionally, once per day you can perform a feat of incredible strength (bend bars, punch through walls, throw heavy objects as weapons).
### Tavern Brawler
**Prerequisites:** Athletics 50+
**Effect:** Improvised weapons gain +1 damage die step and grant access to custom flow actions as if they were a weapon category.
### Victory Lap
**Prerequisites:** Athletics 50+
**Effect:** After defeating an enemy, spend 1 AP celebrating dramatically to gain +10 to your next physical attack or skill check.
## Expert Tier
### Enhanced Reactive Roll
**Prerequisites:** [[#Reactive Roll]], Agility 60+
**Effect:** When using Reactive Roll, you can also move 1 hex directly away without provoking Reaction Attacks.
