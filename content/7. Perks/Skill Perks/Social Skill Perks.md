# Social Skill Perk Descriptions
## Apprentice Tier
### Authority Figure
**Prerequisites:** Influence 40+
**Effect:** Advantage when appearing to be in charge or giving orders to others.
### Crocodile Tears
**Prerequisites:** Deception 40+
**Effect:** Advantage on faking emotions convincingly.
### First Impressions
**Prerequisites:** Influence 40+
**Effect:** Advantage on first impressions with new people.
### Gossip Network
**Prerequisites:** Insight 40+
**Effect:** Learn one interesting secret about anyone after spending 1 hour in their social circle.
### Life of the Party
**Prerequisites:** Influence 40+
**Effect:** Advantage on Influence during meals or social gatherings.
### Matchmaker
**Prerequisites:** Insight 40+
**Effect:** Make an Insight check to identify romantic compatibility between any two people.
### Name Dropper
**Prerequisites:** Influence 40+
**Effect:** Advantage when mentioning connections to important people. **Warning:** Overuse may yield consequences if connections are verified.
### Paper Trail
**Prerequisites:** Deception 40+
**Effect:** Advantage on creating false documentation.
### Technobabble
**Prerequisites:** Deception 40+
**Effect:** Advantage when lying to appear expert on a topic you know little about.
## Journeyman Tier
### Cold Read
**Prerequisites:** Insight 50+
**Effect:** Determine someone's current emotional state, general background, and immediate concerns within minutes of meeting them.
### Cult of Personality
**Prerequisites:** Influence 50+
**Effect:** Inspire fanatical devotion in followers through force of personality.
### Emotional Contagion
**Prerequisites:** Insight 50+
**Effect:** Your mood unconsciously spreads to others around you, influencing the emotional atmosphere.
### Fear Reader
**Prerequisites:** Insight 50+
**Effect:** Advantage to identify someone's fears after spending several hours with them.
### Inspiring Presence
**Prerequisites:** Influence 50+
**Effect:** Spend 2 AP to give all allies within 6 hexes +10 to their next Willpower check.
### Master of Disguise
**Prerequisites:** Deception 50+
**Effect:** Create convincing physical disguises quickly using available materials.
### Rumor Mill
**Prerequisites:** Deception 50+
**Effect:** Advantage on spreading rumors about others.
### Trigger
**Prerequisites:** Insight 50+
**Effect:** Identify what would push mentally unstable people over the edge or bring them back from it.
### Voice Mimic
**Prerequisites:** Deception 50+
**Effect:** Perfectly copy any voice you've heard for at least 10 minutes.
### Web of Lies
**Prerequisites:** Deception 50+
**Effect:** You can maintain multiple false personas simultaneously without slipping up. Additionally, others find it nearly impossible to verify information about your past or identity through normal means.
## Expert Tier
### Connected
**Prerequisites:** Influence 60+, [[Roleplaying Perks#Reputation|Reputation]]
**Effect:** Advantage on Influence checks when dealing with your chosen Reputation group.
### Gaslighting
**Prerequisites:** Deception 60+
**Effect:** Advantage on making others question their own memories and perceptions.
## Master Tier
## Legendary Tier
### Sleeper Agent
**Prerequisites:** [[#Gaslighting]], Deception 90+
**Effect:** Plant false memories or beliefs in someone's mind. Requires 1 hour of interaction and the target must trust you to some extent.
