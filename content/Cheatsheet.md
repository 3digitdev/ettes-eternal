# Basic Resolution
**d100 roll-under system**: Roll ≤ your Skill Level to succeed. 
**Success levels**: 
- Critical (≤05)
- Major (≤¼ Skill)
- Hard (≤½ Skill)
- Normal (≤Skill)
- Failure (>Skill)
- Critical Failure (≥95).
**Advantage/Disadvantage**: Roll twice, take higher/lower result. 
**Opposed Checks**: Compare success levels, higher wins.
# Combat
**Initiative**: d10 + Agility Modifier, act highest to lowest
**Action Economy**: 
- 6 AP and 2 RP per round. 
- Move costs 1 AP per 6 hexes (max 12 hexes/turn). 
- First attack/spell costs 2 AP, second costs 3 AP, third costs 4 AP. 
- Draw/sheathe costs 1 AP each. 
- Taking a Step is free (1 hex, once per turn, no reactions).
**Reactions (1 RP)**: Dodge (Agility), Parry (Melee), Block (Endurance), Reaction Attack. 
**Advanced Reactions (2 RP)**: Riposte, Shield Bash, Intercept
# Damage & Death
**Damage**: weapon/spell dice + Skill Modifier + success bonus (Hard +2, Major +4). 
_Critical hits double total damage._
**Death**: At 0 HP, unconscious and dying. Make death saves (d100 ≤60), need 3 successes in 4 rounds to stabilize.
**Recovery**: Safe option restores 10% max HP, Luck option rolls hit dice equal to Level÷2.
# Flow Points
Start with 2 FP. 
Gain 1 for hits, 3 for critical hits, and 1 for first reaction per round. 
Lose 1 FP at end of turn.
Max FP = Melee/Ranged Skill Modifier.
Spend FP for weapon-specific special abilities (see individual weapons)
# Skill Modifiers
**Skill Modifier** = tens digit of skill (50 skill = +5 modifier). Used for damage bonuses, initiative, health calculations, and maximum Flow Points.
# Spellcasting
**Casting**: Pay AP + Mana costs, make Spellcasting check. Failure wastes AP/Mana. 
**Mana**: Spells cost mana equal to spell tier (Novice=0, Apprentice=1, Journeyman=2, etc).
**Heightening**: +1 Mana per level heightened - maximum highest level you can cast
# Persistent Damage
Take damage at **end of your turn**.  Roll damage each round (if roll). 
**Remove** by spending 2 AP (automatic) OR make skill check:
- Endurance (Bleed/Poison/Shock)
- Agility (Fire/Ice/Acid).
