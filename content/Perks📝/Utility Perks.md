# Utility Perk Descriptions
## Tier 1
### Cat's Grace
Reduce falling damage by {Level} points and always land on your feet.
### Gadgeteer
Once per day, you can modify a mundane item to perform an entirely different function for 24 hours. Examples: turn a mirror into a periscope, a rope into a climbing harness, boots into snowshoes.
### Insomniac
Function normally on 2 hours of sleep; always alert during night watches, immune to sleep effects and dream-based attacks.
### Perfect Pitch
Identify any sound, voice, or musical note; detect sound-based illusions automatically.
### Scavenger
Once per session you can scavenge around you to whip together a working facsimile of a non-magical, non-weapon item no bigger than a backpack. This item will survive for one use or until the end of the day.
### Shortcut
Once per day, find a faster route that cuts travel time to a more helpful amount.
### Time Keeper
You always know the exact time without needing to check any timepiece. Additionally, you can mentally set yourself to wake up at any chosen time while sleeping.
### Urban Navigator
You never get lost in cities; you also instinctively know which areas are safe and which are dangerous.
## Tier 2
### Appraisal
Instantly determine the true value and authenticity of any item. Additionally, identify the most valuable items in any collection at a glance.
### Black-market Contact
You have a reliable contact in the black market who can help you acquire unusual, rare, or illegal items for the right price.
### Expertise
Choose one non-combat skill. When making rolls with that skill, treat your success as one tier higher (failure still fails). Can be taken multiple times for different skills.
### Locksmith
Open locks without proper tools using improvised methods. Additionally, you can quickly assess the security of any building or container, identifying weak points and escape routes.
### Poison Use
You can safely apply poisons to weapons and craft basic poisons.
### Safe House
Once per session, locate or create a secure, hidden location where the party can rest safely for up to 8 hours without being discovered or disturbed.
### Skeleton Key
You can create temporary keys from available materials that will open most common locks. These keys work for 24 hours before becoming useless.
### Translator
Understand the basic meaning of any written language after studying it for a few minutes. Additionally, you can identify the origin and age of documents and inscriptions.
