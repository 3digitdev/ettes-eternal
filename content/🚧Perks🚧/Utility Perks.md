# Utility Perk Descriptions
## Tier 1
### Cat's Grace
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** Reduce falling damage by {Level} points and always land on your feet.
### Gadgeteer
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** Once per day, you can modify a mundane item to perform an entirely different function for 24 hours. Examples: turn a mirror into a periscope, a rope into a climbing harness, boots into snowshoes.
### Insomniac
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** Function normally on 2 hours of sleep; always alert during night watches, immune to sleep effects and dream-based attacks.
### Perfect Pitch
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** Identify any sound, voice, or musical note; detect sound-based illusions automatically.
### Scavenger
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** Once per session you can scavenge around you to whip together a working facsimile of a non-magical, non-weapon item no bigger than a backpack. This item will survive for one use or until the end of the day.
### Shortcut
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** Once per day, find a faster route that cuts travel time to a more helpful amount.
## Tier 2
### Appraisal
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** Instantly determine the true value and authenticity of any item. Additionally, identify the most valuable items in any collection at a glance.
### Expertise
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** Choose one non-combat skill. When making rolls with that skill, treat your success as one tier higher (failure still fails). Can be taken multiple times for different skills.
### Locksmith
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** Open locks without proper tools using improvised methods. Additionally, you can quickly assess the security of any building or container, identifying weak points and escape routes.
### Poison Use
**Prerequisites:** Survival 40+  
**Effect:** You can safely apply poisons to weapons and craft basic poisons.
### Safe House
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** Once per session, locate or create a secure, hidden location where the party can rest safely for up to 8 hours without being discovered or disturbed.
### Translator
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** Understand the basic meaning of any written language after studying it for a few minutes. Additionally, you can identify the origin and age of documents and inscriptions.
