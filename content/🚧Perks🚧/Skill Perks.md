# Skill Perk Descriptions
## Tier 1
### Alchemist
**Prerequisites:** Crafting 30+  
**Effect:** Can craft potions, elixirs, and alchemical items with various beneficial effects.
### Iron Lungs
**Prerequisites:** Endurance 30+  
**Effect:** Hold your breath for 5x longer than normal and are immune to airborne toxins.
### Ward Doctor
**Prerequisites:** Medicine 30+  
**Effect:** You can apply healing effects to 2 people simultaneously with a single Medicine action.
## Tier 2
### Acrobat
**Prerequisites:** Agility 40+  
**Effect:** Move through enemy-occupied spaces without provoking reactions. Additionally, you can use your momentum to perform impossible maneuvers like running across liquid surfaces or bouncing off walls to change direction mid-fall.
### Cold Read
**Prerequisites:** Insight 40+  
**Effect:** Determine someone's current emotional state, general background, and immediate concerns within minutes of meeting them.
### Concealed Cache
**Prerequisites:** Thievery 40+  
**Effect:** When hiding items on your person, increase the difficulty for others to find them during searches.
### Forbidden Knowledge
**Prerequisites:** Any Knowledge skill 40+  
**Effect:** Choose one Knowledge skill. You know dangerous, secret, or lost information that most scholars would never encounter, giving you access to hidden truths.
### Illusion Sense
**Prerequisites:** Perception 40+  
**Effect:** Make an active Perception check to detect illusions of spell level {Level÷2} or lower.
### Magic Item Crafter
**Prerequisites:** Crafting 40+, Spellcasting 40+  
**Effect:** Can craft permanent magical items with various effects and power levels.
### Silver Tongue
**Prerequisites:** Influence 40+  
**Effect:** Once per day, convince someone to do something they normally wouldn't consider, as long as it's not directly harmful to them or their loved ones.
### Strongman
**Prerequisites:** Athletics 40+  
**Effect:** You can lift and carry 3x normal weight limits. Additionally, once per day you can perform a feat of incredible strength (bend bars, punch through walls, throw heavy objects as weapons).
### Tattoo Artist
**Prerequisites:** Crafting 40+, Spellcasting 30+  
**Effect:** Can craft magical tattoos that provide both temporary and permanent effects to recipients.
### Tracker
**Prerequisites:** Survival 40+  
**Effect:** Predict where your quarry is heading based on their behavior patterns and environmental clues. Additionally, you can identify what creatures have been in an area within the last week.
### Web of Lies
**Prerequisites:** Deception 40+  
**Effect:** You can maintain multiple false personas simultaneously without slipping up. Additionally, others find it nearly impossible to verify information about your past or identity through normal means.
### You Saw Nothing
**Prerequisites:** Thievery 40+  
**Effect:** You can remain hidden after taking certain minor actions while maintaining stealth.
## Tier 3
### Flash of Insight
**Prerequisites:**  At least 1 Knowledge Skill 70+
**Effect**:  Once per day, you may increase the success level of a Knowledge Check which you have 70+ Skill Level in.  (Failure → Normal Success → Hard Success → Major Success → Critical Success)
### Mental Fortress
**Prerequisites:** Willpower 50+  
**Effect:** Immune to fear effects and mind-reading attempts. Additionally, you can remain calm and focused during interrogation, torture, or other high-pressure situations without breaking.
### True Sight
**Prerequisites:** Illusion Sense, Perception 60+  
**Effect:** Passively detect illusions of spell level {Level÷2} or lower without needing to roll.
