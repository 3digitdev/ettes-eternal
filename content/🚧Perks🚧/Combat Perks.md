# Combat Perk Descriptions
## Tier 1
### Armchair Tactician
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** Use Academia modifier instead of Agility modifier for Initiative rolls.
### Fleet
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** Your base movement speed increases by 2 hexes.
### Kip Up
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** Standing up from prone costs 1 AP and does not provoke Reaction Attacks.
### Lightning Reflexes
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** Gain +{Level÷2} to Initiative rolls.
### Medium Armor Training
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** You no longer suffer the -10 penalty to Agility, Thievery, Spellcasting, and Athletics from medium armor.
### Quick Thinking
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** Use Willpower modifier instead of Agility modifier for Initiative rolls.
### Shield Proficiency
**Prerequisites:** Endurance 30+  
**Effect:** You become proficient at wielding Shields. You gain access to the Block [[Combat#Reactions|Reaction]], the [[Combat#Basic Reactions & costs|Shield Bash]] Reaction, and to both the Shield Shove and Counter Block [[Combat#Shield|Flow Actions]].
### Weapon Proficiency
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** Choose a weapon category you are not proficient with. You become proficient with that category.
## Tier 2
### Aquatic Warrior
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** You suffer no penalties for underwater combat and reduce armor swimming penalties by half.
### Combat Awareness
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** Gain +1 Reaction Point per turn.
### Dual Wield
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** Remove all penalties for dual wielding weapons.
### Hardy
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** Gain +{Level} additional Health Points.
### Heavy Armor Training
**Prerequisites:** Medium Armor Training  
**Effect:** You no longer suffer the -20 penalty to Agility, Thievery, Spellcasting, and Athletics from heavy armor.
### Intimidating Strike
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** Spend 4 AP to make a Melee attack that inflicts Fear on the target for {Level÷2} rounds on a successful hit.
## Tier 3
### Combat Flow Expertise
**Prerequisites**: None
**Effect**: Gain access to the [[Weapon Categories#Advanced Flow Actions|Advanced Flow Action]] for every weapon you are proficient at wielding.
### Death Defiance
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Effect:** When making Death Saving Throws, treat your roll as one success tier higher. This does not turn failures into successes, but can turn critical failures into normal failures.
