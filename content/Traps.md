# Core Mechanics
- **Crafting Time**: 10 minutes per Tier (Tier 1 = 10 minutes)
- **Materials**: "Trap Materials" cost per trap (see Hunter Field Crafting for daily free allowance)
- **Deployment**: Requires Survival check when placing trap - this sets the Detection/Disarmament difficulty
- **Failed Deployment**: Trap is ineffective and materials are wasted
- **Activation Triggers**: Contact, Manual, Timed, or Magical
- **Evasion**: Most traps allow Agility check when triggered **if detected**; no check if undetected
- **Detection**: Perception check vs deployment Survival roll
- **Disarmament**: Survival check vs deployment Survival roll
- **Duration**: Permanent until triggered (except timed traps)

### Snare Trap
**Materials**: 1 Trap Material  
**Trigger**: Contact  
**Effect**: Target becomes Restrained
**Coverage**: 1 hex  
### Caltrops
**Materials**: 1 Trap Material  
**Trigger**: Contact  
**Effect**: 1d4 damage + Slowed until 2 AP spent removing them OR 3 rounds pass.  If a creature would end its movement on a Caltrops hex, then no Agility Check is possible to avoid this damage. 
**Coverage**: 3 adjacent hexes  
**Detection**: Difficult (-10 to Perception checks)
### Flash Powder
**Materials**: 1 Trap Material
**Trigger**: Manual activation (1 AP) or Timing
**Effect**: All creatures within 2 hexes make Endurance check vs trap's Survival or be Blinded for 1 round
**Coverage**: Blast 2
### Smokescreen
**Materials**: 1 Trap Material  
**Trigger**: Manual activation (1 AP) or Timing  
**Effect**: Creatures inside the smoke are Blind while in the effect, and creatures inside the effect are Invisible to creatures outside the effect.
**Coverage**: Blast 3
**Notes**: Creates concealment, not direct harm
### Spike Strip
**Materials**: 1 Trap Material  
**Trigger**: Contact  
**Effect**: 1d8 damage and 1 Persistent Bleed damage/round.  If a creature would end its movement on a Spike Strip hex, then no Agility Check is possible to avoid this damage.
**Coverage**: Line 3
