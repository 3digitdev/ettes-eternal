# Mental Skill Perk Descriptions
## Apprentice Tier
### Evidence Hunter
**Prerequisites:** Perception 50+
**Effect:** Advantage on Perception checks at crime scenes or investigation scenarios.
### Iron Will
**Prerequisites:** Willpower 50+
**Effect:** Advantage on saves vs mind control and charm effects.
### Paranoia
**Prerequisites:** Perception 50+
**Effect:** Advantage to spot someone tailing or eavesdropping on you.
### Perfect Pitch
**Prerequisites:** Perception 50+
**Effect:** Identify any sound, voice, or musical note; detect sound-based illusions automatically.
### Treasure Hunter
**Prerequisites:** Perception 50+
**Effect:** Successful Perception check tells you the most valuable item in sight at the moment.
### Willpower Clarity
**Prerequisites:** Willpower 50+
**Effect:** Hard Success on Willpower checks lets you see through low-level illusions.
## Journeyman Tier
### Illusion Sense
**Prerequisites:** Perception 60+
**Effect:** Make an active Perception check to detect illusions of spell level {Level÷2} or lower.
### Stubborn Refusal
**Prerequisites:** Willpower 60+
**Effect:** Once per day, automatically resist one effect that would force you to do something.
### Threat Assessment
**Prerequisites:** Perception 60+
**Effect:** Advantage on checks to evaluate combat capabilities of visible opponents.
### Voice Recognition
**Prerequisites:** Perception 60+
**Effect:** Instantly match voices you've heard before to their source, even through disguise or accents.
## Expert Tier
### Mental Fortress
**Prerequisites:** Willpower 70+
**Effect:** Immune to fear effects and mind-reading attempts. Additionally, you can remain calm and focused during interrogation, torture, or other high-pressure situations without breaking.
### Perfect Clarity
**Prerequisites:** [[#Willpower Clarity]], Willpower 70+
**Effect:** Normal Success on Willpower checks sees through low-level illusions.
### True Sight
**Prerequisites:** [[#Illusion Sense]], Perception 70+
**Effect:** Passively detect illusions of spell level {Level÷2} or lower without needing to roll.
