# Novice Spell Descriptions
### Blood Boil
**Level:** Novice  
**Mana Cost:** 0  
**Range:** 8 hexes  
**Target:** One creature  
**Duration:** Instant (Persistent effect)

**Effect:** Target takes Persistent Fire damage per round for Class Tier rounds as their blood temperature rises uncomfortably.

| Class Tier | Persistent Damage | Additional Effect                               |
| ---------- | ----------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Apprentice | 1/round           | -                                               |
| Journeyman | 2/round           | -                                               |
| Expert     | 4/round           | Disadvantage on Willpower checks                |
| Master     | 8/round           | Disadvantage on Willpower and Perception checks |
| Legendary  | 12/round          | Target becomes Frightened of you for duration   |

### Butterfingers
**Level:** Novice  
**Mana Cost:** 0  
**Range:** 8 hexes  
**Target:** One creature  
**Duration:** Sustained

**Effect:** Target has Disadvantage on Thievery, Crafting, and Melee checks while you sustain this curse, as their hands become clumsy and unreliable for fine manipulation.

**Critical Effect:** Target drops one held item immediately.

| Class Tier | Additional Effect                                                                           |
| ---------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Apprentice | Disadvantage on Thievery, Crafting, Melee                                                   |
| Journeyman | Also -1 to all damage rolls                                                                 |
| Expert     | Also -2 to all damage rolls                                                                 |
| Master     | Affects all checks requiring manual dexterity (adds Athletics for climbing, etc.)           |
| Legendary  | Target drops held item on any failed check; on critical they cannot hold anything in hands. |

### Clumsy Curse
**Level:** Novice  
**Mana Cost:** 0  
**Range:** 8 hexes  
**Target:** One creature  
**Duration:** Sustained

**Effect:** Target provokes Reaction Attacks even when Taking a Step while you sustain this curse. Their movements become awkward and obvious.

**Critical Effect:** Target also falls prone.

|Class Tier|Additional Effect|
|---|---|
|Apprentice|Provokes on Step action|
|Journeyman|Also provokes when standing up from prone|
|Expert|Also provokes when drawing/sheathing weapons|
|Master|Also provokes when drinking potions|
|Legendary|Provokes on any non-attack action|

### Evil Eye
**Level:** Novice  
**Mana Cost:** 0  
**Range:** 8 hexes  
**Target:** One creature  
**Duration:** 1 hour or until triggered

**Effect:** Before casting, choose one skill category (Combat, Physical, Mental, Social, Technical, Knowledge). Target has Disadvantage on their next skill check within that category.

**Critical Effect:** Target has Disadvantage on their next two checks within that category.

| Class Tier | Additional Effect                             |
| ---------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| Apprentice | Affects 1 check                               |
| Journeyman | Affects 2 checks                              |
| Expert     | Affects all checks in category for 1 minute   |
| Master     | Can choose 2 categories when casting          |
| Legendary  | Affects all checks in category for 10 minutes |

### Spoilage
**Level:** Novice  
**Mana Cost:** 0  
**Range:** 8 hexes  
**Target:** One creature  
**Duration:** 1 hour

**Effect:** All non-magical food and drink within 1 hex of target goes bad over the next hour. Fresh food rots, water becomes brackish, wine turns to vinegar.

**Critical Effect:** Effect is immediate rather than gradual.

| Class Tier | Radius  | Additional Effect                                     |
| ---------- | ------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| Apprentice | 1 hex   | Over 1 hour                                           |
| Journeyman | 2 hexes | Over 1 hour                                           |
| Expert     | 3 hexes | Over 30 minutes                                       |
| Master     | 4 hexes | Over 10 minutes                                       |
| Legendary  | 5 hexes | Affects magical consumables too (potions, herbs, etc) |

### Stumble
**Level:** Novice  
**Mana Cost:** 0  
**Range:** 8 hexes  
**Target:** One creature  
**Duration:** Instant

**Effect:** Target must immediately move 1 hex in a random direction (roll d6 for hex; 1 is backward and proceed clockwise), provoking Reaction Attacks as normal. If blocked by terrain or creatures, target stops at the obstacle.  Enemies **can** stumble off ledges from this, but get an additional Acrobatics/Athletics Check to avoid falling off.

**Critical Effect:** Target also falls prone after moving; Checks to avoid ledges have Disadvantage.

| Class Tier | Movement | Additional Effect                                                                                  |
| ---------- | -------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Apprentice | 1 hex    | Random direction                                                                                   |
| Journeyman | 2 hexes  | Random direction                                                                                   |
| Expert     | 2 hexes  | You choose direction                                                                               |
| Master     | 3 hexes  | You choose direction                                                                               |
| Legendary  | 3 hexes  | You choose direction, target knocked prone. Critical effect makes them drop what they are holding. |
