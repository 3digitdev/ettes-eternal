# Spell Descriptions
## Alter Self
**Level:** 2  
**Mana Cost:** 2  
**Range:** Self  
**Target:** Self  
**Duration:** Sustained

**Effect:** Minor physical transformation. Change height by up to 1 foot, alter facial features, change hair/eye color, or hide small features.

**Critical Effect:** Changes are permanent until you choose to revert them (no concentration required).

|Class Tier|Additional Effect|
|---|---|
|Apprentice|Basic alterations|
|Journeyman|Basic alterations|
|Expert|Can change apparent ancestry|
|Master|Can change apparent ancestry and gender|
|Legendary|Can mimic specific individuals you've seen|
