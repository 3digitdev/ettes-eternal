# Spell Descriptions
## Arcane Eye
**Level:** 4  
**Mana Cost:** 4  
**Range:** 20 hexes  
**Target:** Floating sensor  
**Duration:** Sustained

**Effect:** Create an invisible floating eye that you can see through. Move it up to 6 hexes per round. It can pass through small openings.

**Critical Effect:** Eye becomes completely undetectable by magical means and can see invisible creatures.

|Class Tier|Movement Speed|Additional Effect|
|---|---|---|
|Apprentice|6 hexes/round|See normally|
|Journeyman|8 hexes/round|See normally|
|Expert|10 hexes/round|Can see in complete darkness|
|Master|12 hexes/round|Can see in complete darkness|
|Legendary|15 hexes/round|Can see through walls (1 hex thick)|

## Black Tentacles
**Level:** 4  
**Mana Cost:** 4  
**Range:** 12 hexes  
**Target:** Blast 4  
**Duration:** Variable

**Effect:** Fill area with writhing black tentacles. Creatures in area are Restrained and take damage each round. Escape requires Athletics or Agility check.

**Critical Effect:** Tentacles also drain 1 mana per round from spellcasters.

|Class Tier|Damage/Round|Duration|Escape Difficulty|
|---|---|---|---|
|Apprentice|1d6 + Spellcasting modifier|4 rounds|Normal Success|
|Journeyman|2d6 + Spellcasting modifier|4 rounds|Normal Success|
|Expert|2d6 + Spellcasting modifier|5 rounds|Normal Success|
|Master|3d6 + Spellcasting modifier|5 rounds|Normal Success|
|Legendary|4d6 + Spellcasting modifier|6 rounds|Hard Success|

## Dimension Door
**Level:** 4  
**Mana Cost:** 4  
**Range:** 50 hexes  
**Target:** Portal between two locations  
**Duration:** Instant

**Effect:** Create a temporary portal between your location and target location you can see or have visited. Portal lasts 1 round and can transport willing allies only.

**Critical Effect:** Prevents enemies from using the portal and allows up to 8 willing allies instead.

|Class Tier|Range|Additional Effect|
|---|---|---|
|Apprentice|50 hexes|Can bring up to 4 willing allies|
|Journeyman|60 hexes|Can bring up to 4 willing allies|
|Expert|70 hexes|Can bring up to 5 willing allies|
|Master|80 hexes|Can bring up to 6 willing allies|
|Legendary|100 hexes|Can bring up to 7 willing allies|

## Illusory Replica
**Level:** 4  
**Mana Cost:** 4  
**Range:** Touch  
**Target:** One object  
**Duration:** 24 hours

**Effect:** Create a tangible duplicate of a touched object. Replica has same weight, texture, and appearance but no magical properties.

**Critical Effect:** Duration becomes 1 week instead of 24 hours.

|Class Tier|Size Limit|Additional Effect|
|---|---|---|
|Apprentice|Small objects (dagger, book)|1 replica|
|Journeyman|Medium objects (sword, shield)|1 replica|
|Expert|Large objects (chest, door)|2 replicas|
|Master|Large objects (chest, door)|2 replicas|
|Legendary|Can create replica without touching original (if you've seen it)|3 replicas|

## Pit of Acid
**Level:** 4  
**Mana Cost:** 4  
**Range:** 12 hexes  
**Target:** 3x3 hex area  
**Duration:** Variable

**Effect:** Create a 2-hex deep pit filled with acid. Creatures that fall in take damage and ongoing acid damage each round they remain in the pit. Climbing out takes 2 AP or 1 AP with successful Athletics check.

**Critical Effect:** Pit becomes 3 hexes deep and creatures have Disadvantage on Athletics checks to escape.

|Class Tier|Fall Damage|Acid Damage/Round|Duration|
|---|---|---|---|
|Apprentice|1d8 + Spellcasting modifier|3 acid|5 rounds|
|Journeyman|2d8 + Spellcasting modifier|4 acid|5 rounds|
|Expert|2d8 + Spellcasting modifier|5 acid|6 rounds|
|Master|3d8 + Spellcasting modifier|6 acid|6 rounds|
|Legendary|4d8 + Spellcasting modifier|8 acid|7 rounds|

## Telekinetic Charge
**Level:** 4  
**Mana Cost:** 4  
**Range:** 12 hexes  
**Target:** One willing ally  
**Duration:** Instant

**Effect:** Magically propel an ally up to their move speed toward an enemy. Their next melee attack gains Advantage and +1 damage die.

**Critical Effect:** Ally can melee attack immediately as part of the movement without spending AP.

|Class Tier|Movement Distance|Additional Effect|
|---|---|---|

|Journeyman|Target's move speed + 2 hexes|+1 damage die|
|Expert|Target's move speed + 4 hexes|+2 damage dice|
|Master|Target's move speed + 6 hexes|+2 damage dice|
|Legendary|Target's move speed + 8 hexes|+3 damage dice|

## Thundercloud
**Level:** 4  
**Mana Cost:** 4  
**Range:** 15 hexes  
**Target:** Blast 3  
**Duration:** Sustained

**Effect:** Create a storm cloud that follows your commands. Cloud can move once per round. Use 1 AP to command it to strike with lightning (maximum 2 attacks per round, Spellcasting check to hit).

**Critical Effect:** Cloud generates 2 lightning strikes per command instead of 1.

|Class Tier|Lightning Damage|Additional Effect|
|---|---|---|
|Apprentice|1d8 + Spellcasting modifier|Cloud moves 4 hexes per round|
|Journeyman|2d8 + Spellcasting modifier|Cloud moves 4 hexes per round|
|Expert|2d8 + Spellcasting modifier|Cloud moves 6 hexes per round|
|Master|3d8 + Spellcasting modifier|Cloud moves 6 hexes per round|
|Legendary|4d8 + Spellcasting modifier|Cloud moves 8 hexes per round, stuns targets for 1 round|
