# Currency
- **100 Copper (c)** = 1 Silver
- **100 Silver (s)** = 1 Gold
- **100 Gold (g)** = 1 Platinum
- **100 Platinum (p)** = 1 Diamond (d)
## Basic Income Reference
- **Average townsperson**: 50s-1g per month (laborers, shopkeepers, craftsmen)
- **Skilled artisan**: 5-10g per month
- **Wealthy merchant**: 50g-1p per month
- **Noble/Lord**: 5+ Platinum per month

# Basic Items

There used to be a table here, with prices and items.  Lots of them.  All the prices were under 1g.  **What's the point.**  

You're heroes, even if you're level 1.  You can afford basic items.  I leave it up to the GM to not let you walk around with a general store on your back, and you still have to declare you _bought_ these things, but my goodness, please don't sit there tracking 10c for a couple torches when you're getting _hundreds or thousands of copper_ worth of gold per adventure.

**GMs:** Be smart.  Let them have nice things.  Track usages, maybe make them expend stuff for tension (_"oh s*** we're out of light sources"_).
