# Stats
- **Hit Dice:** d6
- **Weapon Proficiencies**: 1 of your choice
- **Armor Proficiencies:** None
# Class Equipment
_Pick two at character creation_
- **Curse Focus Crystal** (one sustained spell per day costs 1 less mana to cast)
- **Divination Bones** (roll to gain cryptic but useful information about immediate future, 1/day)
- **Warding Salt** (spend 2 AP to sprinkle in 1-hex circle, advantage on next save vs hostile magic)
- **Familiar Care Kit** (spend 2 AP restore adjacent familiar to full HP instantly, 1/day)
- **Binding Thread** (restrained targets suffer -10 Athletics to escape checks)
# Core Features

| Level | Feature           | Description                                                   |
| ----- | ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1     | Familiar          | Gain a loyal small creature companion with empathic bond      |
| 2     | Forbidden Insight | Study targets to learn secrets for tactical advantage         |
| 3     | Blood Magic       | Sacrifice HP to gain mana at harsh conversion rate            |
| 4     | Bonded Souls      | Enhanced familiar connection with telepathy and shared senses |
| 5     | Path Selection    | Choose path; Journeyman Class Tier                            |
## Familiar
Choose any small creature as your familiar (raven, cat, snake, rat, etc.). Your familiar:
- Can perform simple tasks without AP cost (scout, deliver items, fetch objects)
- Has empathic bond within 100 feet (sense emotions/general state)
- Zero combat capabilities - uses Witch's stats for avoiding attacks/damage
- Has 2 RP of their own to use per round
- **Re-summoning:** 1-hour ritual to re-summon if lost/killed (must be same species)
## Forbidden Insight
Study a target for 5 minutes to learn one secret about them. Choose from:

**Combat Options:**
- **Weakness:** Advantage on your next spell against them this round
- **Vulnerability:** Your spells against them gain +3 damage, +1 round duration, or +2 Persistent damage (whichever applies)

**Non-Combat Options:**
- **Secrets:** Learn either what they want most OR their most important relationship
- **Leverage:** Advantage on Influence checks to manipulate them
## Blood Magic
**Frequency:** Once per day  
Sacrifice HP to gain mana at 5:1 ratio (5 HP per 1 mana gained). This represents dangerous blood magic that weakens you to fuel your spells.
## Bonded Souls
Your connection with your familiar deepens:
- **Telepathic Connection:** Communicate in thoughts at any range
- **Shared Senses:** See and hear through your familiar's senses
- **Health Share:** Choose to take damage instead of your familiar to keep it alive
## Path Selection (Level 5)
At Level 5, Witches must choose their specialization path:
- **Hexweaver Path** specializes in curse magic and sustained debuff effects. They excel at maintaining multiple curses simultaneously and spreading their malefic influence across the battlefield.
- **Blood Witch Path** specializes in life magic and HP manipulation. They excel at using their own vitality as a resource while supporting allies through blood bonds and life transfer.

---
## Hexweaver Path

|Level|Feature|Description|
|---|---|---|
|5|Hex Mastery|Sustain multiple spells and transfer curses from dying enemies|
|6|Extended Hexes|Sustained spells linger for extra round after ending|
|7|Familiar Hex|Familiar becomes mobile curse aura affecting adjacent enemies|

### Hex Mastery
You master the art of layered curses:
- **Multiple Sustaining:** Can sustain 2 spells simultaneously instead of 1
- **Curse Transfer:** When a target with your sustained spell dies, transfer the effect to another enemy within 3 hexes
### Extended Hexes
Your curses resist dispelling through sheer malevolent persistence. Sustained spells last 1 extra round after you stop sustaining them, giving you a buffer to switch between different curse targets.
### Familiar Hex
When casting a sustained hostile spell, you can spend +1 mana to target your familiar instead of an enemy. While your familiar carries this spell, all enemies adjacent to your familiar are affected by the spell for as long as they remain adjacent. The spell cannot transfer on death of affected enemies or the familiar.

## Blood Witch Path

|Level|Feature|Description|
|---|---|---|
|5|Blood Arts|Lifesteal metamagic and HP transfer to allies|
|6|Improved Blood Magic|Enhanced daily uses scaling with class tier|
|7|Blood Reading|Divine information from blood through magical analysis|
### Blood Arts
You learn to manipulate life force as both weapon and gift:
- **Lifesteal Metamagic:** Spend +1 mana on any spell to heal for 25% of damage dealt
- **HP Transfer:** Transfer your HP to willing allies within 6 hexes as a free action
### Improved Blood Magic
Your mastery of blood magic grows with your power. Blood Magic can now be used multiple times per day based on your Blood Witch Class Tier:
- **Journeyman (5-9):** 2 uses per day
- **Expert (10-13):** 3 uses per day
- **Master (14-16):** 4 uses per day
- **Legendary (17-20):** 5 uses per day
### Blood Reading
**Cost:** 2 AP  
**Range:** Touch  
**Check:** Spellcasting skill check

Touch blood to divine information about whoever shed it. Learn information based on success level:

**Information Options:**
1. **Combat Capabilities** - Approximate level, dangerous abilities, resistances
2. **Current Condition** - Injuries, diseases, magical effects currently affecting them
3. **Recent Actions** - What they've done in the last few hours
4. **Emotional State** - Current fears, goals, mental condition

**Success Levels:**
- **Normal Success:** Choose 1
- **Hard Success:** Choose 2
- **Major Success:** Choose 3
- **Critical Success:** Get all 4
