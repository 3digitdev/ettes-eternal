[[Druid🚧#Path of Flora|Flora Path Druids]] gain access to magical seeds that can rapidly grow useful plants. Seeds are a limited daily resource gained through the **Seed Pouch** ability.
# Seed Usage
- **Planting Time:** 1 AP per point to plant any seed
- **Activation:** Most seeds activate immediately upon planting
- **Range:** Seeds must be planted within 1 hex of your location or you must succeed a Ranged Skill Check to place them where you want within 6 hexes
- **Multiple Seeds:** You can plant multiple seeds in the same round if you have sufficient AP
# Seed Categories
## Movement & Terrain
**1-Point Seeds:**
- **Climbing Vines:** Create climbable surface up to 20 feet high for 10 minutes
- **Root Bridge:** Span gaps up to 3 hexes for 10 minutes
- **Slippery Moss:** 2-hex area causes prone checks on movement for 5 minutes
- **Cushioning Fungi:** 3-hex area reduces fall damage by half for 10 minutes
- **Sticky Sap:** 1-hex area halves movement speed for 5 minutes

**2-Point Seeds:**
- **Stone Root:** Create 1-hex raised platform or fill pit, up to 5 feet height/depth, permanent
- **Thorn Barrier:** 3-hex line of difficult terrain for 1 hour
- **Ice Flower:** Create slippery 4-hex area for 30 minutes
- **Anchor Roots:** Prevent forced movement in 2-hex area for 1 hour
## Detection & Communication
**1-Point Seeds:**
- **Tracking Spores:** Mark creature, know general direction for 8 hours
- **Alarm Thorns:** 5-hex perimeter alerts to intrusion for 8 hours
- **Scent Flowers:** +10 to smell-based Perception checks for 10 minutes
- **Echo Moss:** Hear conversations within 20 hexes for 10 minutes
- **Memory Moss:** Plant beforehand, replays 1 hour of activity when collected

**2-Point Seeds:**
- **Message Blooms:** Plant two flowers, send single sentence between them within 1 mile
- **Truth Petals:** Target has Disadvantage on Deception checks for 10 minutes
- **Watchful Vines:** See through existing plant's perspective within 100 hexes for 10 minutes

**3-Point Seeds:**
- **Network Spores:** Two-way communication between up to 4 allies for 1 hour
## Environmental Control
**1-Point Seeds:**
- **Light Moss:** Dim light in 3-hex radius for 1 hour
- **Air Flowers:** 1-hex breathable air pocket for 10 minutes
- **Warmth Berries:** Resist 3 Cold damage for 1 hour (one person)
- **Dry Spores:** Remove moisture from 2-hex area for 10 minutes
- **Fresh Water Vine:** Produce 1 gallon clean water

**2-Point Seeds:**
- **Obscuring Pollen:** Dense concealment in 4-hex area for 10 minutes
- **Sound Dampening Moss:** Advantage on Stealth checks in 3-hex area for 1 hour
- **Purifying Grove:** Remove toxins from 3-hex area permanently
- **Storm Shelter:** Create weatherproof shelter for 4 people for 8 hours

**3-Point Seeds:**
- **Daylight Tree:** Bright light in 10-hex radius for 8 hours
## Restraint & Control
**2-Point Seeds:**
- **Restraining Vines:** Grapple one target for up to 10 minutes
- **Sleep Pollen:** Target makes Endurance check or falls asleep for 5 minutes
- **Confusion Spores:** Target has Disadvantage on attacks for 5 rounds
- **Maze Hedges:** Create confusing 5-hex labyrinth for 30 minutes

**3-point Seeds:**
- **Mass Restraints:** Grapple up to 4 targets in 3-hex area
- **Petrifying Moss:** Turn willing target to stone (reversible) for up to 8 hours
- **Dimensional Anchor Roots:** Prevent teleportation in 10-hex area for 1 hour
## Healing & Support
**1-Point Seeds:**
- **Purifying Flowers:** Remove one poison or disease from ally
- **Nutrition Berries:** Provide full day's food for one person
- **Stabilizing Moss:** Automatically stabilize dying creature

**2-Point Seeds:**
- **Recovery Bloom:** Target gains extra Recovery Roll immediately
- **Antitoxin Grove:** Grant immunity to poison for 8 hours
- **Restoration Fruits:** Remove one negative status effect
- **Healing Circle:** Create 3-hex area; allies gain 1 HP per minute spent inside (up to 10 minutes)
# Enhanced Effects
Starting at Level 14, you can spend additional seeds on the same effect for enhanced results:
- **Double Cost:** Extend duration by 50% or increase area by 1 hex
- **Triple Cost:** Double the normal effect or affect multiple targets where applicable
