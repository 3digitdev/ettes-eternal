# Stats
- **Hit Dice:** d8
- **Weapon Proficiencies**: Polearm OR Mace (pick one)
- **Armor Proficiencies:** Light
# Class Equipment
_Pick one at character creation_
- **Nature speaking staff**: communicate with plants in 10 hex radius, 1/day
- **Nature's wrath seeds**: throw seed to deal {Level÷2} poison damage in 2-hex radius, 3/day
- **Grove marker**: create temporary sacred space 1/day
- **Wildshape focus stone**: extend duration of one transformation by 3 rounds, 1/day
- **Animal calling whistle**: summon one local animal per day
# Core Features

|Level|Feature|Description|
|---|---|---|
|1|Primal Choice|Choose between Animal Companion or Wild Shape|
|2|Nature's Resilience|Daily resistance to one environmental damage type|
|3|Natural Communion|Communicate with natural creatures and request favors|
|4|Primal Casting|Cast self-target spells while Wild Shaped|
|5|Path Selection|Choose path; Journeyman Class Tier|
### Primal Choice
Choose one of the following
#### Primal Choice: Animal Companion
Gain an animal companion from the [[Animal Companions|Animal Companions]] list, with all the standard rules and mechanics
#### Primal Choice: Wild Shape
Choose a single medium-sized land (walking) animal you've encountered before.  You may now use **Wild Shape** to turn into that creature.  If you or the GM are uncertain whether you've encountered a specific non-standard creature in the course of your backstory, make a Nature check (Hard Success required).
##### Wild Shape
**Cost:** 2 mana  
**Duration:** Until voluntarily ended (free action), or you fall unconscious.  **Does not require Sustaining.**
**Effect:** Transform into your previously chosen animal as a Medium Creature.
See [[Druid - Wild Shape|Wild Shape rules]] for more details.
### Nature's Resilience
Each morning during preparation, choose one environmental damage type (Fire, Ice, Shock, or Poison). Gain Resistance 5 against that damage type for the entire day.
### Natural Communion
Communicate basic concepts with any natural creature (animals, magical beasts, plant creatures). Make Influence checks to request simple favors or information from creatures that are not inherently hostile.
### Primal Casting
While Wild Shaped, you can now cast spells that target only yourself. Other spells still cannot be cast while transformed.
## Path Selection (Level 5)
At Level 5, Druids must choose their specialization path:
- **Path of Fauna** specializes in mastery over animals and primal transformation. They gain access to both Wild Shape and Animal Companion abilities, becoming apex predators who hunt with animal allies.
- **Path of Flora** specializes in plant magic and botanical manipulation. They transform their chosen ability into plant-based versions and gain the ability to rapidly grow useful plants through magical seeds.

## Path of Fauna

|Level|Feature|Description|
|---|---|---|
|5|Apex Predator|Gain both Animal Companion and Wild Shape, lose casting while Wild Shaped|
|6|Pack Bond|Animal Companion follows your lead while Wild Shaped|
|7|Small Predator|Can Wild Shape into Small creatures|
### Apex Predator
You gain the Primal Choice option you didn't select at Level 1. You now have both an Animal Companion and Wild Shape ability.

Your Wild Shape ability expands in domain; You can now Wild Shape into ANY medium-shaped land-based creature you have encountered before, chosen each time you cast **Wild Shape**.  **However, you can no longer cast any spells while Wild Shaped, including self-target spells**. Your primal nature becomes too dominant for magical focus.
### Pack Bond
While Wild Shaped, your Animal Companion will automatically follow your lead without commands - moving in the same direction you do and attacking the same enemies you attack. If you want it to perform any other action, it must be Commanded as normal.
### Small Predator
You can now Wild Shape into Small creatures you have encountered. This includes birds, small mammals, and other creatures that can fit through tight spaces or access areas larger forms cannot.

## Path of Flora

| Level | Feature        | Description                                                |
| ----- | -------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| 5     | Plant Form     | Transform chosen ability into plant-based version          |
| 6     | Seed Pouch     | Gain magical seeds for environmental manipulation          |
| 7     | Rooted Casting | Reduce spell costs by remaining stationary and defenseless |
### Plant Form
Your Primal Choice ability transforms into a plant-based version:
- **Plant Shape** (if you chose Wild Shape): Transform into Medium plant creatures - Ent (tank), Thorn Walker (damage), or Moss Shambler (utility). Same mechanics as Wild Shape but with plant creature abilities instead of animal.
- **Plant Companion** (if you chose Animal Companion): Your animal companion becomes a plant creature with similar capabilities but plant-based abilities and immunities.
### Seed Pouch
You gain the ability to rapidly grow useful plants by planting magical seeds. You have **2 seeds per day** at Level 6.  Use of these seeds does not require dirt;  they will grow magically even when used on man-made or processed materials like in towns or on boats.

See the [[Druid - Seed List|Druid's Seed List]] for available seed effects. You start with access to 1-point seeds only.
### Rooted Casting
If you forgo all movement and **relinquish all RP** until the start of your next turn, **all spells** you cast this round cost 1 less AP (minimum 1).  You must not have moved or taken a Reaction this round in order to use this ability.  
_**Exception:** If you have an ability that lets you cast a spell as a Reaction, you may still use this at normal cost;  It does not gain a cost reduction.  For the sake of this exception, assume you have a normal amount of RP._
